Dr. Yuan Longping

The man who addressed global hunger.

The time line of Dr.Yuan Longping's life

  • 1930 - Born at Peking Union Medical College Hospital.
  • 1931-1936 - Lived with his parents in various places.
  • 1949-1950 - Studied at the Department of Agriculture at Xianghui College in Beibei, Chongqing.
  • 1949-1953 Studied agronomy at the Southwest Agricultural College.
  • 1953 - Graduated from the Department of Agriculture at Southwest Agricultural College (now Southwest University).
  • 1960 - He accidentally discovered a special trait of rice in the experimental fields of the agricultural school.
  • 1964 - He found a "natural male sterile plant" in the experimental rice field.
  • 1964-1965 - Yuan Longping's research team conducted hybrid breeding experiments in the rice fields.
  • 1967 - He drafted the "Anjiang Agricultural School Rice Male Sterility Strain Breeding Plan" and reported it to the Provincial Science Committee and the Qianyang Area Science Committee.
  • 1968 - Planted more than 700 precious male sterile seedlings in the No. 7 field of Zhonggu Pan at Anjiang Agricultural School, with an area of ​​133 square meters.
  • 1969 - Yuan Longping, with Li Bihu, and Yin Huaqi went to Yuanjiang County, Yunnan Province to accelerate the propagation of male sterile materials.
  • 1970 - Yuan Longping introduced wild rice from Yunnan, intending to crossbreed in Jingxian (Anjiang Agricultural School moved to Jingxian).
  • 1971 - The Hunan Academy of Agricultural Sciences established a cooperative research group for hybrid rice research, and Yuan Longping transferred to the Hunan Academy of Agricultural Sciences Cooperative Research Group for hybrid rice research.
  • 1973 - The cooperative group found the recovery series through reciprocal crossbreeding and overcame the difficult problem of "three series" matching.
  • 1973, Oct. - Yuan Longping delivered a paper entitled "Progress in the Selection of Three Series Using Wild Rice" at a rice research conference held in Suzhou, officially announcing that the three series of Chinese indica hybrid rice had been matched.
  • 1975 - Yuan Longping overcame the "seed production obstacle" and successfully mastered and summarized seed production technology.
  • 1975, Nov. - Under the strong support of the party and the state, 19 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions successively formed scientific research cooperation groups to carry out mass scientific experiments and successfully bred hybrid rice.
  • 1976 - Hybrid rice was successfully promoted.
  • 1977 - Yuan Longping published two important papers, "Practice and Theory of Hybrid Rice Breeding" and "Key Technologies of Hybrid Rice Seed Production and High Yield".
  • 1980 - After more than 10 years of research experiments, Yuan Longping, a researcher at the Hunan Academy of Agricultural Sciences, successfully overcame the seed production obstacle and achieved comprehensive success in hybrid rice research with the cooperation of relevant scientific research units.
  • 2017 - At the 2017 National Rice New Varieties and New Technologies Display Site Observation Meeting, Yuan Longping announced a new breakthrough in removing heavy metal cadmium from rice.
  • Yuan Longping, known as the "Father of Hybrid Rice," and his team cultivated a super hybrid rice variety "Xiang Liang You 900 (Super Excellent Thousand)" which set a record of 1149.02 kilograms per mu in the test field, as measured by third-party experts.
